AI in Everyday Life

AI isn’t just a futuristic idea — it’s part of your daily routine right now. From the way your social media app decides what to show you, to how your phone’s voice assistant understands you, AI is everywhere.

Recommendation systems on platforms like TikTok or Netflix use AI to suggest content you’ll like based on your behavior.

AI powers smart home devices, improves online shopping experiences, and helps businesses automate tedious tasks.

Understanding AI means understanding the tools shaping the future — and the opportunities it creates for innovation and creativity.
