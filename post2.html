How AI Sees the World

Unlike humans, AI doesn’t “see” images the way we do. Instead, it processes pixels and patterns in data.

Using techniques like computer vision, AI algorithms analyze images by detecting edges, shapes, colors, and textures. They can recognize faces, objects, and even emotions by learning from millions of labeled examples.

This allows AI to do things like unlock your phone with facial recognition, filter your social media feed, or even help doctors detect diseases from scans.

The magic is in how AI models learn to interpret the vast amounts of data they’re given, turning raw information into meaningful insights.
